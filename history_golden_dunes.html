UNDER THE DUNES OF THE SAHARA : THE STORY BEHIND GOLDEN DUNES

AN INVITATION TO THE HEART OF THE DESERT:

The Sahara, that vast, windswept golden expanse, is much more than a landscape. It's a source of infinite inspiration, a place of mystery and contrast where the heat of the sun meets the cool of the night. Through Golden Dunes, we wanted to capture the very essence of this majestic environment: a meeting between the raw power of nature and the bewitching gentleness of its secrets.

NOTES THAT TELL A STORY :

Golden Dunes opens with amber and spicy notes, reminiscent of the markets of Marrakech, where spices exhale their aromas into the warm air. This is followed by a heart of golden honey, reminiscent of shady oases and sweet teas shared under a Berber tent. In the background, deep, mysterious cedarwood, evokes caravans crossing the dunes under a Starry sky.
